About Elli Beauty

Elli Beauty is all about creating products that are 100% natural, organic and safe for bodies. Our products moisturize the skin while healing and protecting damaged skin with nutrients and vitamins from plant food without any chemicals that clog pores in the skin. Our Lotion helps to relieve dry skin by replenishing the skin's water content and locks in moisture in the top layer of the skin which in turn treats dryness and allows the skin to heal and return back to being healthy and hydrated. Our product is water-based and free from preservatives and has both hydrating and regenerative properties. We strongly believe that what you put inside your body is as important as what you put on your body. Our products here at Elli Beauty promotes the use of herbal plants and science to promote healthy skin. Our unique range of natural ingredients include plant extract organic oils that sooth and heal the skin.
